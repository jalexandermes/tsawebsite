FCCLA

What is FCCLA?

FCCLA (Family, Career and Comsumer Leaders of America) is a student-led organization for those intrested in leadership, community service, and careers in family and consumer sciences. Members build skills in areas like cooking, child development, fashion, and financial literacy while making a positive impact through service projects, competitions, and teamwork.

FCA

What is FCA?

FCA (Fellowship of Christain Athletes) is where many Christains come together and praise the Lord.This club is for many to either learn about the Lord or grow their relationship with him.

FBLA

What is FBLA?

FBLA (Future Buisness Leaders of America) is a club where students are taught about many life skills such as graphic design, marketing, and more. FBLA is a good club for those people wanting to learn about real world necessary things like jobs.

FFA

What is FFA?

FFA (Future Farmers of America) is a program where students learn about farming and agruculture. Students at FMMS have many things that they can do and learn about such as showing pigs, harvesting plants in a green house, and more.

FMNN

What is FMNN?

FMNN (Feagin Mill News Network) is a student-led organization where students can teach others about what is happening in FMMS and do many fun things like diffrent segments. Currently there are 6 segments: Main News, Deep Thought Thursday, Sources of Strength, Joke, Mean Muggin Monday, and the Weather Brothers.

TSA

What is TSA?

The Technology Student Association (TSA) is a national organization for midle school and high school students intrested in STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math). Through hands-on projects, competitions, and leadership opportunities, TSA helps students develop problem-solving, teamwork, and technical skills to prepare for future careers in technology and engineering.
